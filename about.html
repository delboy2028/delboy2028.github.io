About

DivisionBuilds.github.io is an unoffical, non-commercial, open-source, fanmade website for the community of the game Tom Clancy's The Divsion.

The ultimate goal of this website is to allow users to easily share and find builds. Furthermore, we aim to assist them in build creation and theory crafting by providing stat calculators.

Keep in mind that this website is still work in progress. Layout and content may be changed multiple times during the development process. If you want to know more about the development process, follow @Division_Builds on Twitter or join the DivisionBuilds Discord: discord.me/divisionbuilds.
